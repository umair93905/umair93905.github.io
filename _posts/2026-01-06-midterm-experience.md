title: "My Midterm Experience"
---

After attending classes for a few weeks, it felt like everything was going smoothly. However, very soon, I was introduced to the reality of the semester system when my midterm exams were announced. It all happened so quickly that I felt unprepared and stressed. Since I had never experienced a semester system before, it was difficult for me to adjust to such a fast academic pace.

As the exams approached, I became very worried because I felt that I had not prepared enough. Unlike the annual system in college, where we had a lot of time, here everything was happening in a short period. I tried my best to cover the syllabus, but due to lack of experience, I could not manage my preparation effectively.

One of the most difficult moments for me was the Discrete Mathematics exam. Unfortunately, the night before the exam, my health was not good, and I could not prepare properly. As a result, my performance in that paper was very poor. This made me feel very disappointed and stressed because I knew it would affect my overall result.

After completing all the midterm exams, I realized that I needed to improve my study habits and time management. I understood that university life requires consistent effort rather than last-minute preparation.

Although my midterm performance was not very good, it became a turning point for me. Instead of losing hope, I decided to learn from my mistakes and focus on improving for the final exams. This experience taught me an important lesson: failures are not the end, but an opportunity to grow and improve.
