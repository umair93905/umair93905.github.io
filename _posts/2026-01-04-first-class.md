title: "My First Class"
---

My first day of classes was filled with nervousness and excitement. As I entered the classroom, I saw many students already sitting there. Just like me, they were also new and seemed a bit nervous. Seeing unfamiliar faces made me realize that we were all starting a new journey together.

The first class we attended was a tutorial session, which was designed to give us an introduction to our field. In this session, we learned about Computer Engineering, its scope, and what we would study in the coming semesters. This helped reduce my confusion and gave me a clearer understanding of my chosen field.

After that, we had a few more classes on the same day. Since it was the first day, most teachers focused on introducing themselves and explaining the course outlines. They told us about the subjects, grading system, exams, and expectations.

Although there was no heavy academic pressure on the first day, I still felt nervous because everything was new. However, as the day progressed, I started feeling more comfortable.

This experience helped me understand that university life is very different from school and college. It requires more independence, responsibility, and focus.

Overall, my first class was a mix of nervousness, curiosity, and excitement. It marked the beginning of my academic journey at the university.
