---

After attending a few classes, I had my first Programming Fundamentals lab, which was a very important experience for me. Since I was a Computer Engineering student, I quickly realized that programming would be one of the most important subjects in my field.

When I entered the computer lab with my classmates, I felt a bit nervous. There were computers all around, and I realized that my skills in using computers were not very strong at that time. This made me feel slightly uncomfortable, but I was also excited to learn something new.

In the lab, our instructor introduced us to the basics of Programming Fundamentals. We were taught about the programming environment and how to install Visual Studio. Setting up the environment was a new experience for me, and I found it very interesting.

As the lab progressed, I started understanding how programming works. Even though I faced some difficulties in the beginning, I was eager to learn. Slowly, my confidence started improving as I practiced more.

This first lab session played a crucial role in building my interest in programming. It made me realize that although I was a beginner, I could improve with practice and dedication.

Overall, my first programming lab was both challenging and exciting. It marked the beginning of my journey in coding and helped me take my first step toward becoming a better programmer.
