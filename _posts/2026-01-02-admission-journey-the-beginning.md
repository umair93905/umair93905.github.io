title: "My Admission Journey: The Beginning"
---

After completing my intermediate education, I entered one of the most important phases of my life: deciding my future career and university path. At that time, I did not have a clear idea about which specific field I wanted to pursue, but I was determined to continue my education in a professional field.

During my college years, I had already shifted to ICS, which introduced me to computer-related subjects. This gradually developed my interest in technology and engineering. However, I was still exploring my options and trying to understand the best direction for my future.

The turning point came when our mathematics teacher informed us that students who wanted to pursue engineering would have to appear in entry tests conducted by different universities. This was a completely new concept for me, and I had very little knowledge about it. After hearing this, I went home and started searching online to understand the entry test system, its format, and preparation methods.

Initially, out of curiosity and without any preparation, I applied for the NUST NET-1 test. I was very nervous at that time because the test fee was high and I had no idea about the paper pattern. I took the test just to experience the environment and understand its difficulty level. I scored 81 out of 200, which was not a strong result, but it gave me valuable insight into how entry tests work.

After this experience, I realized the importance of proper preparation. I joined an online academy, Pass Education System, and started preparing seriously alongside my 12th class studies. Managing both board exams and entry test preparation was not easy, but I remained consistent and focused.

Due to financial constraints and travel difficulties, I could not attempt more NUST tests, but I applied to other universities such as UET, FAST, PIEAS, GC University Lahore, and the University of Sargodha. During this time, my 12th class studies were also continuing, which made the situation more challenging.

This phase of my journey was full of stress and uncertainty. I had applied to multiple universities, each with different fee structures. Some universities had very high fees, while others were more affordable. Since my financial background was not very strong, I had to carefully consider my options.

Despite these challenges, I tried to remain patient and hopeful. I discussed my situation with my family, who supported me throughout this difficult time. I realized that uncertainty is a natural part of every journey, and one must stay strong and focused.

Finally, after a long period of waiting, I received an offer letter from UET Lahore while I was in Islamabad for the PIEAS entry test. I had been selected for admission in the Faisalabad campus under the A2 category. Following the advice of my teachers and family, I submitted my documents and paid the fee before the deadline.

After about a week, the second merit list was announced, and fortunately, my category was upgraded to A1 in Computer Engineering at UET Faisalabad. This was a moment of great happiness for me and my family. All the stress, hard work, and patience finally paid off.

This admission journey taught me valuable lessons about patience, hard work, decision-making, and resilience. It marked the beginning of a new chapter in my life.
