title: "My First Semester Result"
---

After the final exams, the next phase was waiting for the result. I was very anxious because I knew that my performance in the midterms, especially in Discrete Mathematics, had not been good. Even though I had improved in the finals, I was still worried about the overall outcome.

When the result was announced, I found that I had received a C+ grade in Discrete Mathematics, and my overall GPA was 2.94. At first, I felt disappointed because I had hoped for a better GPA. For a moment, I even felt like giving up, thinking that my efforts were not enough.

However, instead of staying negative, I decided to talk to my friends and teachers. I shared my concerns with them, and they encouraged me a lot. My teachers told me that 2.94 is not a bad GPA and that I still have time to improve in future semesters.

Their support helped me regain my confidence. I realized that one semester does not define my entire future. What matters is how I learn from my mistakes and improve myself.

In the end, I accepted my result and decided to move forward with a positive mindset. This experience taught me the importance of patience, resilience, and self-belief. It also motivated me to work harder in the next semester.
